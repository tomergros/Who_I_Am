- About me: -

I am Oriel Somech, an Identity management system administrator and a Computer Science Student. I served in the army in the air force.

I am interested in expanding on my fascinating work in an Identity Management System:

IDM - Identity Management System is a system whose role is to manage the identification of the various users in the organization and their access permissions to the various resources (for information systems, infrastructures, managed files) in the organization. The first central facet of the IDM system is the management of users and authorization profiles for the systems managed in the organization from one central place and the making of the necessary settings in the various connected systems, directly from the identity management system in a mechanized and automatic manner. Also, the system has the ability to perform duty separation through rules. The other aspect is that the system allows for central password management, enforcement of password changes and password synchronization for the various systems. On the third side, the system monitors the relationship between users and resources and enables a wide range of user account management operations and permissions in the various systems. A clear illustration of the system's operation is the efficient and effective handling of granting permissions to new users (employees) on the one hand, and the efficient and effective closing of the permissions to users who have stopped their operation (employees who have left).
